Guest DJ Signups

Every semester we have the Guest DJ Challenge, where DJs of any skill level (beginner to pro) have a chance to throw a party at Level B. Each Guest DJ is given a Tuesday to play the entire night (10-1Am) with the goal to have fun and bring a bunch of people to Level B to hear your music! Each semester, we award the DJ with the highest sales Guest DJ Night $300 and opportunities for some DJ's to play at Level B on a regular night! Click here to learn more.

About

Level B is an inviting Bar, Lounge, and Dance-floor whose generous cocktails, dedicated staff, and plush seating area transport you to another level. Come enjoy smooth early evening cocktails and stay to dance the night away. Level B is located in the heart of Collegetown, and very close to downtown Ithaca, at the corner of Dryden Road and Eddy Street. Coming from downtown, Level B is just up to the left of the top of Buffalo Street, next to Miyakes. Take your nightlife to another level.
